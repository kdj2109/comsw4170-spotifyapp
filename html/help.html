Help

Our app is here to help you, the user, keep up with all the news from your favorite artists. You can search for your favorite artists, track and untrack them and even listen to their music, all within our app! Tracking an artist adds their news content to your feed and adds all of their buzz to your personalized ‘My Artists’ page. On the ‘My Artists’ page you can look at the news you saw on the newsfeed, as well as blog posts and reviews, all while listening to their music! We hope that our app is easy to use but for those just getting started here are some helpful hints!

Search Artist: To search an artist simply type their name into the search bar on the top of the page. A scrollable dropdown will be autopopulated with artists that match your search. Simply click their name and you will be brought to their artist news page.

Track Artist: To track an artist click next to their name on their artist page after searching them. You can also track them directly from the search bar dropdown by clicking . They will now appear in your newsfeed and on your ‘My Artists’ page.

Untrack Artist: To untrack an artist navigate to your page. Click to edit your list and click to remove them from your list. Finally, click to confirm your edits. You can also untrack them through the search bar dropdown or from their artist page by clicking . Untracked artists will no longer show up in your newsfeed or on your ‘My Artists’ page.

Play Music: To play music navigate to your page. Select the artist you want to listen to from your list of tracked artists. In their content box navigate to the ‘Play Artist’ tab. Select a song and play! Note that you need the Spotify desktop app installed to be able to use this widget.
